1. Push notifications (app)

To turn push notifications on or off on your device:

  • iOS: Settings → Notifications → select the New Car app, then enable or disable notifications.
  • Android: Settings → Notifications → App notifications → New Car (wording may vary by device).

Menu names may differ slightly depending on your OS version.
